Mountain photos for the ski log
===============================

Only FEATURED mountains (featured: true in data/travel.js) get the big
full-bleed photo panels with the frosted name inside the image. Drop a
wide image here for each, under the filename in its img: field:

  zermatt.jpg        Zermatt
  dolomiti.jpg       Dolomiti Superski
  deer-valley.jpg    Deer Valley
  bachelor.jpg       Mt Bachelor
  banff.jpg          Banff (Lake Louise / Sunshine / Norquay)
  niseko.jpg         Niseko

Tips: landscape orientation, at least ~1900px wide (the panel spans
the whole viewport), sky or open space in the upper third so the
frosted name reads well. Until a photo exists the panel shows a
styled ridgeline placeholder.

Everything else in SKI (featured or not) appears in the "Every
mountain" master list below the panels and as a snowflake pin on the
globe's ski view. To feature a different mountain, just move the
featured: true flag (and give it an img:).

Want the name to sit BEHIND the peaks (true masking, like the Soelden
reference)? That needs the photo itself edited - drop the originals
in here and ask Claude to cut the skyline masks.
